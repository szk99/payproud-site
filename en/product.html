Our Product

PayProud / Wenxin Wukui

A digital utility app that makes personal records, goals, and progress feedback lighter, clearer, and more rewarding.

Not just another ledger app

PayProud is not positioned as a simple bookkeeping app. It is a digital tool designed around perception, feedback, and goal momentum, turning daily records into a clearer and more sustainable experience.

Core Features

Income and Asset Visualization

Make income growth and accumulation easier to see so that each step forward feels tangible.

Daily and Monthly Progress Feedback

Use immediate feedback to understand where you are today and how your month is moving overall.

Wishlist Goal Tracking

Turn a target into a visible path you can keep moving toward with momentum and satisfaction.

Installment Plan Management

Organize installment plans more clearly so complex arrangements feel easier to follow and manage.

Income and Expense Records

Keep the input experience lightweight while preserving enough structure to review past records clearly.

Themes and Display Preferences

Support a more personal visual experience so the product feels both useful and closer to individual habits.

Use Cases

Professionals who want to see their accumulation

View monthly progress more clearly and give everyday effort a stronger sense of feedback.

Young users with clear savings goals

Set a goal worth reaching and stay motivated as you move toward it step by step.

Users managing installment plans

Bring scattered payment plans into one clearer daily view that feels easier to manage.
